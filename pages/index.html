Introduction

Hello! Our names are Christopher Vu (cg3sp20fw) and Amy Le (cg3sp20dq), and we wanted to focus our final project on 2020 and some of the current issues we're facing at the moment. Unless you've been living under a rock, you've probably been in quarantine just like us for the past few months due to the COVID-19 pandemic. Even though we're all facing tough times, we wanted to address some things that you can still do to get by during quarantine. We included some infographics above from the World Health Organization that can help you understand the COVID-19 pandemic better. This includes ways to communicate with friends and family, recipes to test out, and hobbies that you might want to pick up! Also, if you're interested in our process of making this website, you can view the progress that we've been making on the project github.
